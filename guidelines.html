Guidelines

Prescribed rules and regulations for Students & Parents

ATTIRE

Wear prescribed uniform suggested by Natyalaya. Contact the Administrative executive of Natyalaya for queries.

Uniform tops

Yellow

Legins

Pink

Hair

  • Braided
  • Tied as a bun
  • Secured

Footwear

Barefoot or with socks

BAGGAGE

Items to carry in your bag for daily class. Only Natyalaya supplied Classmate Notebook is allowed to maintain uniformity.

Class notebook

Capture notes. Inform instructor in advance when you run out of pages.

Pen, Pencil & Eraser

To write notes in theory class .

Water Bottle

To keep you hydrated

Hand towel

To refresh or dry sweat

CLASS ROUTINE

Things to follow in the class room.

Arrival

10 mintues before the class

Training

Learn with concentration & take necessary notes what the instructor teaches.

Discipline

Maintain discipline inside the class room. Ask the Guru or the instructor for any help.

Leave

Mark your attendance & carry your belongings when you leave.

ABSENTEEISM

The Guru will advice on the extra work needed to catch up with other students due to the absence.

Report in advance

· Call or text to Instructor mobile

  • Email msnatyalaya@gmail.com

PICKUP & DROP

Parents are responsible for pickup & drop but incase of personal inconvenience, alternate contact should be given.

Communicate alternate authorized contact

· Inform instructor about the alternate escort contacts.

· Natyalaya will verify the contact & send the kid.
