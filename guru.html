About The Guru

Kousalya Nivas's performances and achievements.

About the Guru

Bharata Natyam, the supreme art form which skillfully embodies the three primary ingredients of dance - BHavam, RAgam and THalam, fascinated Mrs. Kousalya Nivas right from toddlerhood. She started to learn dance at the age of 4. She has done nearly 300 stage performances. She has been performing professionally for the past 25 years and is one of the most sought after dancers of her generation. She has bagged more awards in state level and national level competition in Bharata Natyam. She brings to her dance a rare sense of devotion and dedication, which leaves the audience with a sense of spiritual fulfillment.
Mrs. Kousalya Nivas is a well-trained dancer on Bharata Natyam and also on other forms of dances. She is a disciple of following Gurus:
  1. Mrs. Revathi Muthuswamy, Srirangam
  2. Late Mrs. Indra Kannan, Srirangam
  3. Mr. VijayaKumar, Coimbatore.
Currently she is pursuing her Masters in Bharata Natyam, Annamalai University under Guru Mrs.Pushkala Ramesh, Srirangam.
Endowed with a pleasing stage presence, Kousalya Nivas brings to her audience a refreshing presentation of Bharata Natyam, exhibited through her strong sense of rhythm, scintillating nritta and lively abhinaya. Everyone who has seen Kousalya Nivas perform is struck by her unique thought process and fresh approach to dance. While deeply rooted in tradition she has evolved her own individual style without affecting the core principles of Bharata Natyam.
Mrs. Kousalya Nivas is basically an engineering postgraduate. She completed her B.Tech in Information Technology in Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, Coimbatore during 2008. She started her career as a Software engineer at Wipro. Then she switched to teaching profession working as a Lecturer in CMRIT, Bangalore because teaching is her passion. Later, she took a break from work and pursued M.Tech in Computer Networks & Engineering in CMRIT, Bangalore & completed the degree with University topper award during July 2014. Also, she bagged the “BEST STUDENT” Award for 2007 during her UG program from amongst the members of ISTE Students Chapter, New Delhi at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, Coimbatore.
Mrs. Kousalya Nivas hopes that as she strives towards greater excellence in dancing, her passion for Bharata Natyam and other forms of art will endow her with courage to break boundaries and widen her horizons in all avenues of life. Her vision is to pass on the varied culture and glorious heritage of this wonderful art to the young and upcoming generation. To accomplish this, she started her own dance school M.S.NATYALAYA in Marathahalli, Bangalore during 2012. She is training nearly more than 80 students. She has dedicated herself in spreading this beautiful art to the people. She acknowledges her sincere thanks to her Gurus for their expert guidance and her parents & in-laws who have identified and greatly encouraged her in making her dreams a reality. She greatly appreciates her husband and son without whose cooperation and support, pursuing her passion & running this institute would have been impossible.
