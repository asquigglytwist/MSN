Welcome to
M.S.Natyalaya

A Bangalore based Bharatanatyam institute at Chinnappanahalli, Marathahalli.

We, M.S.Natyalaya, are a Bangalore based Bharathanatyam institute. We impart the knowledge of Bharatanatyam to our students by several degrees of training, so that they learn the fundamentals in-depth and get involved in the art with full devotion and dedication. Having a thorough understanding, they not only excel in dance but also contribute to the development of the art.
