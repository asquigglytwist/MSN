About M.S. Natyalaya

MSN

M.S. Natyalaya is a Bangalore based institute for Bharathanatyam. Our vision is to preserve the traditional art form by offering an in-depth training in both theory and practical.
Founded in 2012 with only a handful of students, due to the quality of the training offered, we've grown to currently train more than 90 (Ninety) students.
M.S.Natyalaya also has tie up with Annamalai university, Chidambaram one of the renowned university for fine arts through which M.S.Natyalaya offers various grade exams & certificate programs for the students to get certified in specific skillset at various levels.
