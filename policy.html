Privacy Policy & usage Terms and Conditions

M.S.Natyalaya would like to let all the visitors to our website know that we are concerned, as you are, about the privacy of any personal information that you may provide to us through this website.
You are not required to provide any personal information through this website unless you choose to do so. We cannot guarantee confidentiality of e-mail, and therefore recommend that you do not send confidential information via e-mail.
By providing your name, address and any additional information you freely consent to M.S.Natyalaya, holding, using and processing your personal data for the purposes set out by the nature of our business and on this website. Any personal information you provide to M.S.Natyalaya via this website in relation to any request for, or download of information or other services provided by M.S.Natyalaya may be retained and used by us.
This information may be used by M.S.Natyalaya for the purpose of evaluating your feedback, to provide response and and to communicate on critical updates, process improvements & other announcements.
If for any reason you are concerned that the personal information maintained at M.S.Natyalaya is not correct or have any questions about privacy policies, please contact us at management@msnatyalaya.com
